At Young Strong Adventurous We Have 3 Main Passions That Motivate and Drive us everyday.

  1. To feed the orphans and widows and visit them in their distress. This is True Religion
  2. To educate them and to give them not only hope of a better future but the opportunity to suceed in life. Providing them with the education, resources and skills necessary to be confident and compitent so that they excel in the field of their choosing. This in turn adds immense value to their life that you cannot put a price tag on. Things like direction, purpose, self esteem and the ability to provide for themselves.
  3. To take them on adventures around the world that will open their eyes to not only to a greater persepctive but new thoughts and a new understanding of the world around them.
